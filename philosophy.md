Categorize apps for a "dumbphone" - a phone for intentional living that blocks passive consumption and endless scrolling.

  PHILOSOPHY: Allow tools that help you DO things (communicate, navigate, create, work, stay healthy). Block apps designed for passive consumption (scrolling feeds, watching videos, browsing products).

  ALLOW - Apps that serve you:
  • Communication: WhatsApp, Slack, Discord, Teams, Telegram, email, video calls (Zoom/Meet)
  • Health & Wellness: Strava, AllTrails, Calm, meditation, affirmations, motivational quotes, fitness trackers
  • Music & Audio: Spotify, Pandora, Apple Music, podcast apps, Shazam, music production tools
  • Essential Services: Banking, payments (Venmo/PayPal), healthcare, pharmacy, insurance
  • Productivity: Calendar, notes, Todoist, Notion, AI assistants (ChatGPT/Claude)
  • Creative Tools: Photo/video editing, Canva, Google Photos, design apps
  • Transportation: Uber, Lyft, maps, Waze, transit apps, DoorDash, food delivery
  • Travel & Events: Airbnb, Hotels, Ticketmaster, OpenTable, Yelp, recipe apps
  • Utilities: Weather, calculator, smart home (Google Home/SmartThings), vehicle apps, browsers
  • Tracking/Logging: Letterboxd, Goodreads, activity logs

  BLOCK - Apps that consume you:
  • Social Media: Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, Reddit, Snapchat, Pinterest, BeReal, social drawing platforms (PENUP)
  • Video Streaming: YouTube, Netflix, Hulu, Disney+, all video platforms (even educational)
  • News: NYTimes, CNN, news apps, crime apps (Citizen)
  • Shopping: Amazon, eBay, Target, Walmart, Etsy, all retail/marketplace apps
  • Gambling: Casinos, sports betting, DraftKings, FanDuel
  • Gaming: ALL games including simulators (golf games, racing), puzzles (Chess, Wordle, Sudoku, coloring by number), game creation platforms (Castle), hyper-gamified apps (Duolingo, Habitica)
  • Sports Entertainment: ESPN, live scores, fantasy sports
  • Dating apps
  • Content platforms: Medium, Substack, Wattpad

  Spotify → ALLOW (music)
  YouTube → BLOCK (video)
  Strava → ALLOW (fitness)
  Instagram → BLOCK (social media)
  Google Maps → ALLOW (navigation)
  ESPN → BLOCK (sports entertainment)
  Shazam → ALLOW (music utility)
  Amazon → BLOCK (shopping)
  Calm → ALLOW (wellness)
  TikTok → BLOCK (social media)
